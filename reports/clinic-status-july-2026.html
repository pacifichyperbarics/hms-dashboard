Responsibility-driven clinic management

The clinic model is intentionally decentralized. Monica, Rudy, Deb and the other clinic leaders are motivated by meaningful responsibility and direct ownership of outcomes. The objective is not to dilute that responsibility with unnecessary layers.

HMS should preserve individual accountability while providing clear authority, measurable outcomes, central billing and administrative support, emergency backup coverage, cross-training and freedom to solve local operating issues within an approved framework.
